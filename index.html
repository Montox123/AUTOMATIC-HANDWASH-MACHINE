AUTOMATIC HANDWASH MACHINE

In these time, we are facing a lot of problem due to Corona virus, many shopping malls, food plaza , restaurants, shops are closed and the economy of all over World is falling down.The people can't afford sensor based product and they have to shut there restaurants, food store, shops etc. If they are opening, they are not using the good practices, which leads to the spread of corona virus as the taps of the wash basins are touched by every people and no one knows who is contaminated with the virus and this is a global issue now. In that situation I thought of building something which will be helpful for our society. I will build a cost efficient AUTOMATIC HANDWASH MACHINE which will fit on the existing taps of the wash basins of Restaurants and Shopping

WORKING

It works with the help of servo motor, arduino, sensor. The sensor will detect the hand which is shown by human and the motor will rotate anticlockwise to open the tap of wash basin, and as per requirement the tap will close after 20 sec or 15 sec.

ADVANTAGE

The following product will prevent people from touching the taps that will reduce the spread of corona. This will make people's life easy and corona free at some extent and also save some water of our Mother Earth.

SERVO MOTOR

Servo motor works on the PWM ( Pulse Width Modulation ) principle, which means its angle of rotation is controlled by the duration of pulse applied to its control PIN.

POWER ADAPTER

A power supply for electronic devices. Also called an "AC adapter" or "charger," power adapters plug into a wall outlet and convert AC to a single DC voltage.

LITHIUM ION

A lithium-ion battery or Li-ion battery (abbreviated as LIB) is a type of rechargeable battery.Here Lithium-ion batteries are used to provide power supply to the arduino.

JUMPER WIRE

Jumper wires are simply wires that have connector pins at each end, allowing them to be used to connect two points to each other without soldering.
